About Milwaukee Brewers

The Milwaukee Brewers are an american professional baseball team and are based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. The team is named for the city's association with the brewing industry. The team was founded in 1969 as the seattle pilots, an expansion team of the american league, in Seattle, Washington. After only one season, the team relocated to Milwaukee, becoming known as the Brewers and playing their home games at Milwaukee County Stadium. In 1998, the Brewers joined the National League and they have one world series appearance in 1982.

Wisconsin is a U.S. state located in the north-central United States. It is the 23rd largest state by total area and the 20th most populous. The state capital is Madison and its largest city is Milwaukee. Wisconsin is knows as "America's Dairyland" because it is one of the nation's leading dairy producers.
